Privacy & Data Collection

Prompt Intent does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Your prompts, questions, and all interactions remain completely private on your device. I built this extension with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought.

Zero Data Collection

I do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your prompts and interactions remain completely private.

On-Device Processing

All processing happens locally on your device using Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano AI. Nothing ever leaves your computer.

No External Servers

No external servers or APIs are contacted. Your data is never sent to the cloud or any third-party services.

No Tracking

No analytics, tracking, telemetry, or personal information is accessed or stored. Your privacy is guaranteed.

How it works: All AI processing is powered by Gemini Nano, Chrome's on-device AI model. This means everything happens directly in your browser without any internet connection. Your prompts never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
